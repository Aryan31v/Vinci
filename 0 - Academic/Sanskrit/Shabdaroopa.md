## ЁЯза THE ANALYST BREAKDOWN
> [!info] Concept Simplified (ELI5)
> Declensions (Shabdaroopa) are like **outfits** that a noun wears to show its role in a sentence (who is doing the action, to whom, with what, etc.).

### ЁЯз▒ Pattern Recognition
- **Dvivachan (Dual):** Always follows a 3-2-2 pattern (Prathama/Dwitiya same, Tritiya/Chaturthi/Panchami same, Shashthi/Saptami same).
- **Bahuvachan (Plural):** The 'рднреНрдпрд╛рдореН' (bhy─Бm) and 'рднреНрдпрдГ' (bhyaс╕е) endings are consistent across most nouns.

> [!tip] Modern Correlation (Linguistics)
> **Ayurvedic Concept:** Vibhakti 
> тЖФ **Modern Equivalent:** Case Endings (Nominative, Accusative, etc.)
> **Key Insight:** Understanding Vibhakti is essential for decoding the precise medical instructions in Samhitas (e.g., dosage, timing).
