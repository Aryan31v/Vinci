## üß† The Brain (Encephalon)
### 1. Forebrain (Prosencephalon)
- **Telencephalon:** Cerebrum (Cerebral Cortex, White Matter, Basal Nuclei).
- **Diencephalon:** Thalamus, Hypothalamus, Epithalamus, Subthalamus.

### 2. Midbrain (Mesencephalon)
- Connecting link between Forebrain and Hindbrain.
- **Parts:** Tectum (Colliculi), Tegmentum, Cerebral Peduncles.

### 3. Hindbrain (Rhombencephalon)
- **Pons:** Bridge between parts of the nervous system.
- **Medulla Oblongata:** Vital centers for respiration and cardiac function.
- **Cerebellum:** Coordination of voluntary movements and balance.

## üêç The Spinal Cord (Medulla Spinalis)
### Extent
- From **Foramen Magnum** to **L1/L2 Vertebra**.
- Continues as **Conus Medullaris** and **Filum Terminale**.

### Segments (31 Pairs)
- 8 Cervical
- 12 Thoracic
- 5 Lumbar
- 5 Sacral
- 1 Coccygeal

### Internal Structure
- **Grey Matter:** H-shaped central core (Horns).
- **White Matter:** Peripheral tracts (Ascending & Descending).
