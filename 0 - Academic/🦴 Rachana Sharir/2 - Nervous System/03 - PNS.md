## üß∂ Nerve Plexuses
### 1. Cervical Plexus (C1-C4)
- Supplies neck and diaphragm (Phrenic nerve).

### 2. Brachial Plexus (C5-T1)
- Supplies the Upper Limb.
- **Key Nerves:** Radial, Median, Ulnar, Axillary, Musculocutaneous.

### 3. Lumbar Plexus (L1-L4)
- Supplies Anterior Thigh.
- **Key Nerves:** Femoral, Obturator.

### 4. Sacral Plexus (L4-S4)
- Supplies Posterior Thigh, Leg, and Foot.
- **Key Nerves:** Sciatic, Tibial, Common Peroneal.
