## ðŸ”¬ The Neuron
Structural and functional unit of the nervous system.
- **Cell Body (Soma):** Nucleus, Nissl granules.
- **Dendrites:** Receive signals (Afferent).
- **Axon:** Transmits signals (Efferent).

### Types of Neurons
1.  **Multipolar:** One axon, many dendrites (Most common, Motor).
2.  **Bipolar:** One axon, one dendrite (Retina, Olfactory).
3.  **Unipolar:** One process dividing into two (Sensory ganglia).
