## üè• Common Conditions
### 1. Stroke (Cerebrovascular Accident - CVA)
- **Ischemic:** Blockage of blood flow (Thrombus/Embolus).
- **Hemorrhagic:** Rupture of blood vessel.
- **Hemiplegia:** Paralysis of one side of the body (opposite to lesion).

### 2. Lumbar Puncture
- Collection of CSF for diagnosis.
- Site: L3-L4 or L4-L5 interspace (below termination of spinal cord).

### 3. Bell's Palsy
- LMN lesion of Facial Nerve (VII).
- Facial asymmetry, inability to close eye, drooling.

### 4. Hydrocephalus
- Accumulation of excess CSF.
- Causes increased Intracranial Pressure (ICP).
