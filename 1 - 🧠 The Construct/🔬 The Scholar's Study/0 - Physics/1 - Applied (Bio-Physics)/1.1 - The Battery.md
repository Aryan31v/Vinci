> [!abstract] Physics Concept: Capacitance & Voltage
> Your body doesn't "use" electricity like a wire; it **stores** it like a battery (or more accurately, a capacitor).
> **The Goal:** Create a voltage difference (Potential) so that when a signal comes, the energy is *already there*, ready to explode.

## ðŸ”‹ The Biological Capacitor

In physics, a **Capacitor** consists of two conductive plates separated by an insulator (dielectric).
- **Plate A (Outside):** Positive Charge (+)
- **Plate B (Inside):** Negative Charge (-)
- **Dielectric (The Wall):** The Cell Membrane (Lipid Bilayer).

**The Result:** Voltage.
A typical neuron holds a charge of **-70mV**.
This sounds small, but across a microscopic membrane, it creates an electric field strong enough to align proteins.

### ðŸ§ª The Chemicals (Charge Carriers)
Unlike copper wires which use electrons, biology uses **Ions** (Wet Electricity).
1.  **Sodium ($Na^+$):** The "Outside" Crowd. Want to get in. High pressure.
2.  **Potassium ($K^+$):** The "Inside" Crowd. Chilling inside.

## âš™ï¸ How the Battery Charges (The Pump)
Entropy wants equilibrium. It wants to mix everything up ($Na^+$ and $K^+$ equally distributed = 0mV = Dead Battery).
To fight Entropy, your body uses a machine: **The $Na^+/K^+$ Pump**.

- **Cost:** It burns ATP (Energy) constantly.
- **Action:**
    - Kicks **3 $Na^+$ OUT**.
    - Pulls **2 $K^+$ IN**.
- **Net Math:** You lose 1 positive charge every cycle.
- **Result:** The inside becomes negative relative to the outside. **The Battery is Charged.**

## ðŸ§  Why This Matters (The "Gun" Metaphor)
A neuron isn't just "passing" a message. It is **loaded**.
- **Resting Potential (-70mV):** The gun is cocked. The hammer is pulled back.
- **Action Potential:** You don't "push" the bullet; you just pull the trigger (open the channels). The stored energy does the rest.

> [!tip] Scholar's Insight
> Anxiety is often described as "Pent-up energy."
> Biologically, this is literal. Your nerves are "Hyper-Polarized" or "Hyperexcitable," holding too much charge, ready to fire at the slightest trigger (noise, movement, thought).
> **Bio-Hack:** "Grounding" or deep slow breathing reduces this background voltage, making the trigger harder to pull.
