### The Formula: Samyama
Patanjali describes a specific mental technology called **Samyama**. This is the simultaneous practice of three stages of focus:
1.  **Dharana (Concentration):** Fixing the mind on a single object (like a candle flame, a deity, or a concept).
2.  **Dhyana (Meditation):** The flow of attention becomes unbroken, like oil pouring from a jug.
3.  **Samadhi (Absorption):** The observer disappears. You become so merged with the object that only the object exists in your awareness.

### Mechanism of Action
Patanjali states that if you perform Samyama on a specific object, you gain intimate knowledge of it.

> *"By samyama on the light within, one gains knowledge of the subtle, the hidden, and the remote."* â€” Sutra 3.26

**The Pratyahara Gateway**
*Pratyahara* (sense withdrawal) is crucial. When external perception shuts down, internal perception activates.
